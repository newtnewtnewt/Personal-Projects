Newtilocks' Arrival

On a very sunny day in the middle of the woods, a family of three newts were eating breakfast together and laughing the day away. There was Momma Newt, Papa Newt and Baby Newt all together. After breakfast, the family decided to go out for a morning walk to enjoy the sunny, but cool weather. Minutes after they had left, a young boy named Newtilocks, also on a walk for the day, found himself in front of the Three Newts' Cabin. Curious about who was inside, he wandered in the door that was unlocked. After all, this was the Midwest, no one locked their doors.




Newtilocks and the Newt House

Newtilocks was never a troublemaker, but he was hungry and tired from a long day walking. When he came to the table in the dining room, he found 3 breakfast burritos partially eaten. At first he tried Papa Newt's burrito. This one has too much ketchup in it he exclaimed! Next he tried Momma Newt's burrito. I cannot believe the lack of salt in these eggs, this is practically inedible Newtilocks was discouraged but tried the final burrito, Baby Newt's. Finally a breakfast burrito worth eating Newtilocks then ran over to the couch and went to talk a nap, because who doesn't like couch naps? Unfortunate for Newtilock's luck, the three newts had returned home. Newtilocks sprung awoke from the couch, and was confronted by all three newts, looking very angry."




Newt-olution

Newtilocks had a brief recollection of a story like this long ago, but he couldn't remember all the details quite right. The newts were all very angry looking, but after he explained how tired and hungry he had been with his MyFitnessPro Tracker and FitBit logs for the day, the Newt family saw exactly where he had been coming from. They welcomed him into the house for the remainder of the nice, cool, sunny day and enjoyed the company of each other for many years to come. The ending, much like any good kid's story should end, was indeed happily ever after. THE END.
